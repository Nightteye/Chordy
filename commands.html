/addartist

Music

Add an artist to your favorites collection

Usage

/addartist <artist_name>

Examples

/addartist Taylor Swift /addartist The Weeknd

Information Returned

  • Confirmation of artist added
  • Artist details and image
  • Total followers count
  • Add to favorites list

Usage

Examples

  • Related artists
